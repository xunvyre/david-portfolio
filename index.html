About
Me

As an artist turned web developer, I have experience with both sides of the process and can assist from conception to finished product. I am an Adobe Certified Associate in Photoshop and I use the program almost daily. I have a passion for crisp lines, tiny sculptures, clean code, and native wildlife! In addition to the color pink, I draw influence from a family history in Computer Science, growing up with Disney and Studio Ghibli, and my love for nature.

I currently reside in sunny Florida with my best friend, nine fat guinea pigs, and our fishtank. When I'm not coding or making art, I'm learning new recipes or playing video games! ... Or taking a nap.

My favorite food is chicken satay and I'm a hobbyist sticker and pin collector!
