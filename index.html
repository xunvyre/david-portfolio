About Me

As young artist turned web developer, I have experience with both sides of the process and can assist from conception to finished product. I have a passion for crisp lines, tiny sculptures, clean code, and native wildlife! In addition to the color pink, I draw influence from a family history in Computer Science, growing up with Disney and Studio Ghibli, and my love for nature.

I currently reside in sunny Florida with my best friend and nine guinea pigs. When I'm not coding or making art, I'm practicing cooking or (most often) playing video games!
